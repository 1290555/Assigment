    Our services

    At Saylani Welfare Trust, we focus on serving people in every way we can. Our main services include:

  • Food support:Providing daily meals to thousands of deserving individuals and families.

  • Healthcare:Offering free medical treatment, hospitals, and clinics for those who cannot afford it.

  • EducationRunning schools, colleges, and vocational training programs to build a brighter future for the       youth.

  • Financial Assistance:Helping struggling families with monthly support, ration, and emergency aid.

  • Skill Development:Training people in IT and technical fields so they can earn a livelihood with dignity.

  • Disaster Relief:Reaching out to affected communities during natural disasters and crises.
